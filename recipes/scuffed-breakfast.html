Scuffed Breakfast

Description

The signature scuffed breakfast is a meal that is really easy and cheap to make but not the healthiest or most nutritious meal that you could have.

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of ramen
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 hashbrown patty

Steps

  1. Set water to a boil
  2. Add in your ramen and cover your container for 5 minutes
  3. Add 2 eggs into a greased pan and cook them over easy
  4. Put your hashbrown into the air fryer or oven at 400 degrees for 15 minutes (flip halfway)
  5. Stir your ramen and drain the water
  6. Add 1 egg onto your plate
  7. Add the cooked ramen on top of your egg
  8. Add the second egg on top of the ramen
  9. Take out your hashbrown and add it to the plate
  10. Enjoy your meal!
