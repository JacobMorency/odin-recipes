Breakfast Sandwich

Description

A simple breakfast meal that is fast and easy. Also known as an EggMcMuffin from McDonalds.

Ingredients

  • English muffin
  • 3 strips of bacon or a sausage patty
  • 1 egg
  • 1 slice of cheese

Steps

  1. Butter your pan(s)
  2. Cook the egg to your liking as long as it fits on your choice of bread
  3. Cook your bacon/sausage patty
  4. Once everything is cooked layer your ingredients as followed: McMuffin Bottom, Egg, Cheese, Bacon/Sausage Patty, McMuffin Top
  5. Enjoy Your Meal!
