Egg Fried Rice

Description

Egg fried rice is a simple and easy to make meal that is widely used by people trying to meal prep on a diet.

Ingredients

  • 2 or 3 eggs
  • 1 cup of cooked rice
  • A handful of mixed vegetables
  • Soya sauce (optional)

Steps

  1. Spray or butter your pan
  2. Add your eggs
  3. Constantly stir
  4. Add in your mixed vegatables
  5. Keep stirring
  6. Add in your cup of cooked rice
  7. Add soya sauce (optional)
  8. Stir until everything is cooked
  9. Enjoy your meal!
